Machu Picchu Diorama in UE5


This theme was picked randomly from a wheel of themes. Then went on to plan and design my 3D environment. All textures are made in photoshop apart from the pottery which was created in blender. I had great fun designing this and researching about Machu Picchu to see what I could include in this diorama.

I utilised vertex colours on the wheat and grass to make it flow with the wind node in unreal engine. I also created all the assets inside of blender and then hand drew the stone effect in photoshop.
