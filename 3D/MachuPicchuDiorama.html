Machu Picchu Diorama

This is a diorama that was created for the first project in my games development course in college.

UE5 Blender Photoshop

Machu Picchu Diorama in UE5

This theme was picked randomly from a wheel of themes. Then went on to plan and design my 3D enviroment. All textures are made in photoshop apart from the pottery which was created in blender. I had great fun designing this and researching about Machu Picchu to see what I could include in this diorama.
