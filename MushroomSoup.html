Mushroom Soup

This is a game I created for the Untitled Game Jam #84; one of my first games jams I ever entered.

This game was made with Blender and UE5. The art was created by me inside Photoshop. The aim of the game is for you to collect mushrooms for your soup, but there is some "entitys" lurking in the fog waiting for you...
