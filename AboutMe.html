Education


I studied computer science in secondary school as one of my GCSEs this made me want to get into coding. To do this I went to college to study BTEC Level 3 in Creative Media Practice - Games Development. In which I used Blender, Unreal Engine Blueprints, Substance Painter and much more to create and design two game projects. I am currently pursuing university to continue my Games Development journey.

Experience


I have around 2 years of experience inside UE5 and have a strong understanding of the blueprint system. Using industry-standard tools such as Bender, Substance Painter and Photoshop I have created concept art to games. I have also used tools such as Asprite, Qubicle Voxel Editor and much more to aid this. I love learning new things and experimenting with them. I have made a few games to date, most of them I made for game jams where I try and develop my skills. I have learnt Unreal Engine Blueprints, but I am trying to delve into C++ and C#.

My Free Time


In my free time I usually try to copy other game mechanics to understand how they work. This is great to understand how games may have created their mechanics and figure best practices for coding mechanics.
